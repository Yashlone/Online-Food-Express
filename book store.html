Welcome to Word Palace!

We are a family-owned and operated bookstore that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We offer a wide variety of books, including fiction, non-fiction, children's books, and more. We also have a large selection of magazines and newspapers.

We are proud to offer our customers a wide variety of books at competitive prices. We also offer a variety of services, including gift wrapping, book clubs, and author events.

About Us

Word Palace was founded in 2007 by Singh Brothers. They opened Word Palace with the goal of providing the community with a great place to buy books.

Since opening, Word Palace has become a popular spot for both locals and tourists. The bookstore has received rave reviews from critics and customers alike.

Singh Brothers are proud to be part of the community, and they are committed to providing their customers with a great shopping experience.
